Notes
• Zero gaps — faces are flush to each other. All values confirmed by pixel scanning.
• PT 2.0 shirts have no torso back face — only R, F, L. Roblox back face is dropped on conversion.
• Arm faces: y=71–360 (h=290). Top caps y=7–69. Bottom caps y=362–424.
• Pants waist: y=519–808 (h=290). Leg faces: y=661–952 (h=292).
