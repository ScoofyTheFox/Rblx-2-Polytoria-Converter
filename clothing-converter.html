Notes — scanned pixel-by-pixel from template.png
• Zero gaps. Faces are flush to each other. All values confirmed by scanning exact first/last colored pixel per column.
• PT 2.0 shirt has no torso back face — only R, F, L. Roblox back face dropped on conversion.
• Arm faces: y=71–360 (h=290). Top caps y=7–69 (h=63). Bottom caps y=362–424 (h=63).
• Torso R starts y=103; F and L start y=104. All end around y=391–392.
• Pants waist: y=519–808 (h=290). Leg faces: y=661–952 (h=292). Leg caps: h=63.
